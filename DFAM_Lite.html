The DFAM Lite

WELCOME!
THE DFAM LITE IS AN INTERPRETATION OF THE "DFAM" FROM MOOG WHICH IS A STEP SEQUENCER MEANT TO MIMIC DRUM NOISES USING SYNTHESIZED SOUND.
IN ORDER TO GET STARTED YOU FIRST NEED TO HIT THE "INITIALIZE SOUND" BUTTON BELOW. THIS WILL START THE OSCILLARS AS WELL AS THE WHITE NOISE GENERATOR.
AFTER THAT, PRESS THE "START/STOP" BUTTON, WHICH WILL START THE SEQUENCER, AND THEN SLIDE THE MASTER VOLUME UP TO AN APPROPRIATE VOLUME FOR YOUR SYSTEM.
FROM THERE YOU CAN USE ANY OF THE SLIDERS OR SWITCHES BELOW IN ORDER TO CREATE A SOUND THAT YOU LIKE.
TO RECORD SOUND, JUST HIT THE "START RECORDING" BUTTON. THIS WILL CAPTURE THE SOUND STARTING FROM THE MOMENT THE BUTTON IS PRESSED.
ONCE DONE, JUST HIT THE "STOP RECORDING" BUTTON AND A DOWNLOAD LINK WILL APPEAR UNDER THE "INITIALIZE SOUND" BUTTON.
CLICK THE LINK TO DOWNLOAD YOUR VERY OWN "DFAM_LITE_RECORDING.WAV" FILE
ENJOY!
